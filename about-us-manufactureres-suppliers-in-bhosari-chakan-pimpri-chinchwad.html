ABOUT INDUSTRIAL MINERALS & REFRACTORIES

WHO WE ARE

We take this opportunity to introduce ourselves M/S Industrial Minerals & Refractories as one of the leading suppliers of Refractory Fire Bricks, Insulation Bricks & Insulation Material, Refractory Mortars & Castables, Minerals, etc in Maharashtra State , India since last 40 Years. In all these years we have earned from our customers is their trust on us and because of this we are still associated with our customers from the past two decades. Through reliable products, we will help our customers to minimize plant shutdowns, maintenance costs and heat losses and maximize their operational efficiency, productivity & earnings. INDUSTRIAL MINERALS & REFRACTORIES was incorporated to consistently offer refractory materials in India & abroad, with good manufacturing practices, assured deliveries and impeccable services, so as to ensure complete customer satisfaction. We are regularly Exporting to Dubai, Syria, Kenya, Nigeria and New Zealand and are very much interested in expanding our Export activities. We are also Importing “FOAM FILTERS” from “SQ Group”, CHINA who are No1 of the Largest Manufacturers of Foam Filters in the world. With this introduction we welcome you to the qualified league of businessmen specializing on the commodities listed above. Let the dialogue begin with the exchange of ideas and markets know how, which definitely would give way to a point of revenue generation. We also have a SERVICES WING by the name of “REFRACTORY APPLICATION & SOLUTIONS” and we are carry out all Kinds of Activity with subject to “REFRACTORY DISMANTLINING & RELINING” ALONG WITH “INSULATION WORK”

OUR STRENGTHS

  • We have our own manufacturing at Gujrat where the firing of the Refractories is done in an Oil Fired Tunnel Kiln, and we have a Heavy Duty Press and fully equipped Pattern shop and for achieving stringent quality parameters we have now Quality Control Department, which is, equipped with latest laboratory facility for testing Raw material & Finished Products.
  • We provide for detailed Inspection for the commodities and in case of Exports we also provide Inspection by one of the best Inspection Agencies viz SGS etc.
  • Effective Logistics management through affiliations with the best of CHA services in the region.
  • Proven expertise in Procurement chain management with direct collaboration with the Producers and Processors. This advantage places us way ahead of others supplier and exporters when it comes to sourcing the products of best quality.
  • Well-equipped, well-informed and knowledgeable administration staff is our strength.
  • We also impart suggestion in selecting Quality Refractories and Insulation Material which is suitable to your application and which helps in reducing the fuel consumption.
  • We have been catering to almost all steel Plants in Pune and we expertise in supplying Special Shape Refractories as per Customers Drawing & Specification & we supply in all Qualities right from AL- 30% to PCPF & ULC Quality.
  • The Quality Assurance department works hand in hand with the operations. The team is involved right from checking incoming raw material to in Process Checks to Final Testing and Inspection. All procedures like grinding, mixing, blending and maintenance of Kiln Furniture are closely monitored and studied.
